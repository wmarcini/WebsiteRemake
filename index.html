Chicago Pet Rescue is a non-profit orginization that is foster home and volunteer-based rescue. Dedicated to rescuing stray and abandoned animals. We do not have a central "shelter" as most of our animals are placed in foster homes until forever homes can be found.

Our goal is to rescue as many animals as we can safely manage through foster homes. We rescue dogs, puppies, cats, kittens, birds and other small animals.

We believe in supporting businesses, as they support us in so many ways.
